Using a spreadsheet

An easy way to obtain the required eqation for your CSS calc() is to create a sheet in your favourite spreadsheet app like Google Sheets.
Simply enter the (x,y) values for points p1 and p2 like shown and create a line-diagram with those values. Add a 'trend-line' and show it's equation (which is actually all we need).
